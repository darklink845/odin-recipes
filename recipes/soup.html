Soup

Description:

Soup is a very simple meal that involves boiling vegetables in water until their flavors and nutrients have been extracted.

Ingredients:

  • Kale
  • Onion
  • Tomatos
  • Carrots
  • Vinegar

Steps:

  1. Pour water into a saucepan and boil it
  2. Add in onions and carrots
  3. Add in tomatos
  4. Add in kale
  5. Add in vinegar
  6. Serve!
