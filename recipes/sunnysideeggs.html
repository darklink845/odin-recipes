Eggs

Description:

Sunny side eggs are a very simple but delicious meal to make, great as a quick breakfast when you're in a hurry but need something nutritious.

Ingredients:

  • 2 Eggs
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Heat oil to medium heat in a small saucepan
  2. Crack eggs into a bowl
  3. Once the saucepan is heated, pour the eggs onto the saucepan
  4. Occasionally spoon oil onto the eggs until the whites are cooked through and completely opaque.
  5. Enjoy
