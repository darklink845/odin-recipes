Lasagna

Description:

Lasagna involves sandwiching bread between cheese in several layers. This makes for a filling dinner fun for the whole family!

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Crushed Tomatos
  • Italian seasoning

Steps:

  1. Boil pasta water
  2. Cook ground beef
  3. Cook vegetables
  4. Make tomato sauce
  5. Layer sauce between pasta noodles in a baking pan
